Plain-language summary: We collect what's needed to run your account and games (profile info, gameplay stats, technical data), use cookies and analytics to keep the Platform working and improve it, never sell your personal data, and give you ways to access, correct, export, or delete it.

01 Information We Collect

We collect information in three ways: what you give us directly, what we collect automatically as you use the Platform, and what we receive from third parties (such as a payment processor confirming a purchase).

Information you provide

  • Registration details — username, email address, password (stored as a salted hash, never in plain text), and optional profile information (avatar, bio).
  • Content you create — messages, published games, comments, and support requests.
  • Payment-related details when you subscribe or make a purchase, handled by our payment processors (see Third-Party Services) — Eylox does not store your full card number.

Information collected automatically

  • Gameplay data — scores, achievements, session length, in-game actions, and coin/currency balances.
  • Device and technical data — IP address, browser type, operating system, language, and approximate location derived from your IP.
  • Usage data collected via cookies and similar technologies, described below.

02 Account Information

Your account information (username, email, hashed password, subscription status, and Eylux balance) is used to authenticate you, secure your session, deliver the features you've paid for, and communicate with you about your account (such as security alerts or billing receipts). You can review and update most account information from your Profile and Settings pages.

We use secure, server-generated session tokens rather than storing your password in your browser. Sessions can be revoked instantly — for example, if you change your password or report unauthorized access.

03 Cookies

We use cookies and similar local-storage technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in and remember your session between visits.
  • Remember preferences such as display theme, sound settings, and language.
  • Measure performance and understand how features are used, as described in Analytics.

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings. Blocking essential cookies may prevent you from staying signed in or using certain features of the Platform.

04 Analytics

We use analytics tools to understand aggregate trends — such as which games are most played, where errors occur, and how features perform — so we can improve the Platform. Analytics data is generally pseudonymous or aggregated and is not used to make decisions about you individually outside of the Platform's normal gameplay and moderation systems.

Where we use a third-party analytics provider, that provider is bound by the terms in Third-Party Services and only processes data on our behalf.

05 Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) between your device and our servers.
  • Hashed, salted password storage — we never store or display your plain-text password.
  • Opaque, revocable session tokens instead of long-lived credentials in the browser.
  • Rate limiting and monitoring to detect abuse, credential stuffing, and unauthorized access attempts.

No system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable law.

06 Children's Privacy

Eylox is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children below that age without verifiable parental consent. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under the applicable age without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal information to us without consent can contact us using the details in Contact Information.

07 Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Platform. After you delete your account, we delete or anonymize personal information within a reasonable period, except where we need to retain certain data:

  • To comply with legal, tax, or accounting obligations.
  • To resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, or investigate fraud and Terms violations.
  • In backups, which are cycled out on a rolling schedule.

08 Third-Party Services

We share limited data with service providers who help us operate the Platform, under contracts that restrict their use of your data to providing services to us. These may include:

  • Cloud infrastructure and database providers, who host our application and store account and gameplay data.
  • Payment processors, who handle subscription billing and Eylux purchases and receive the payment details necessary to process your transaction.
  • Analytics providers, described in Analytics.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may disclose information if required by law, to protect the rights and safety of Eylox or our users, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to standard confidentiality protections.

09 Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete your account and associated personal information.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, including some analytics.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before completing certain requests.
