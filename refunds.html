Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026 · Applies to all purchases made on Eylox

Plain-language summary: Subscription refunds are available within a short window of a charge if you haven't heavily used the benefits; Eylux purchases are final once delivered, with a few fraud/error exceptions. Reach out and we'll review every request individually.

01 Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, a request generally must meet all of the following:

  • Submitted within 14 days of the original charge date.
  • Related to a purchase made directly through Eylox (not through a third-party app store, if applicable — those are refunded per that store's own policy).
  • Not associated with an account that was suspended or terminated for violating our Terms of Service.
  • Not a purchase already substantially used, as detailed per product type below.

Meeting these criteria does not guarantee a refund — each request is reviewed individually, and we may grant exceptions in either direction based on the circumstances.

02 Premium Refunds

EYLOX Premium subscriptions can be refunded if you request within 14 days of a charge and have not substantially used the billing period's benefits (for example, claimed the monthly Eylux bonus or redeemed exclusive cosmetics tied to that period). Refunding a subscription charge:

  • Cancels the current billing period immediately and removes Premium benefits from your account.
  • Does not retroactively remove Eylux already earned through normal gameplay during that period.
  • Does not affect future renewals, which you can cancel separately from your account settings at any time.

03 Creator Pass Refunds

Creator Pass follows the same 14-day window as Premium, with one additional condition: a refund will generally not be granted if you have published a game, earned creator revenue, or otherwise actively used Creator Pass-exclusive tools (such as the advanced AI game builder or analytics dashboard) during the billing period being refunded. If you've used these features, we may offer a partial or pro-rated refund instead of a full one, or decline the request, depending on the extent of use.

04 Eylux Purchases

Eylux coin purchases are final and non-refundable once the coins have been credited to your account, because they can be spent immediately and are difficult to claw back after use. We make limited exceptions where:

  • The purchase failed to deliver Eylux to your account due to a technical error.
  • You were charged more than once for the same purchase (duplicate charge).
  • The purchase was made fraudulently by someone other than you, and you report it promptly.

If your Eylux balance from a disputed purchase has already been spent, we may reverse the specific in-game items or benefits obtained with it as part of resolving the refund, where technically possible.

05 Processing Time

Once a refund is approved:

  • We process the refund on our end within 2–3 business days.
  • Your bank or payment provider may take an additional 5–10 business days to reflect the refund in your account or statement, depending on their own processing schedules.
  • You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund has been issued from our side.

If it has been longer than 10 business days since you received our refund confirmation and the funds haven't appeared, contact your bank first, then reach out to us if the issue persists.

06 Exceptions

Beyond the standard rules above, we evaluate the following situations case by case:

  • Accidental purchases — for example, a purchase made by a child without permission, or an obvious duplicate click — are usually eligible even if slightly outside the standard window.
  • Billing errors caused by Eylox (incorrect price charged, subscription not cancelled despite a timely request) are always eligible regardless of the 14-day window.
  • Chargebacks filed with your bank instead of contacting us first may result in a temporary suspension of your account's paid features while the dispute is investigated, and may affect your ability to use paid features in the future.
  • Legal requirements in your country may grant you refund rights broader than this policy — where that's the case, the stronger local right applies.
