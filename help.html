❓ Help Center

Find answers to common questions about Eylox

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Visit the login page and click "Create Account". Enter your desired username and password, then confirm. You'll be ready to play immediately!

Can I play as a guest?

Yes! Click "Play as Guest" on the login page to start playing without creating an account. Your progress will be saved locally.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Follow the recovery instructions sent to your email.

Games & Gameplay

How do I earn coins?

Play games and complete missions to earn coins. The better you perform, the more coins you'll earn. Daily missions offer bonus rewards!

What are the 3D games?

Check out our 3D games like Eylox Obby, Treasure Island, Pirate Adventure, and City Explorer for immersive gameplay experiences.

Can I play offline?

Most games require an internet connection. However, your progress is saved locally and synced when you reconnect.

Leaderboard & Ranking

How does the leaderboard work?

The leaderboard ranks players by their total wins across all games. Check back daily for new rankings!

How do I increase my ranking?

Win games to increase your score. Each personal best in a game counts as a win. Challenge yourself across different game types!

Friends & Clans

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends section and search for usernames. Click "Add Friend" to send a request.

How do I create a clan?

Visit the Clan Wars section and click "Create Clan". You can invite friends to join your clan and compete together!

Still need help?

Contact our support team or check the Safety & Privacy page for more information.
