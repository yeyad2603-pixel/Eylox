🛡️ Safety & Privacy

Your safety and privacy are our top priority

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Eylox ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the Eylox gaming platform. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information Collection and Use:

  • Account Information: username, password (encrypted), email address
  • Profile Data: avatar, coins, wins, games played
  • Game Progress: scores, achievements, session data
  • Usage Data: gameplay analytics, feature usage

We use this information to provide and improve our gaming services, personalize your experience, and maintain platform security.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • Encrypted password storage using bcrypt
  • Secure local storage of user preferences and game progress
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

User Rights & Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data anytime in your profile
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of optional data collection features
  • Request an export of your game progress

To exercise these rights, visit your account settings or contact our support team.

Community Guidelines

To maintain a safe and enjoyable gaming environment, all players must:

  • Treat other players with respect
  • Avoid harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory behavior
  • Not share personal information with other players
  • Not attempt to hack, cheat, or exploit game systems
  • Not spam or engage in commercial solicitation

Violations of these guidelines may result in account suspension or permanent ban.

Age Restrictions

Eylox is intended for users age 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete such data and terminate the child's account.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us through the Help Center or email our support team.
