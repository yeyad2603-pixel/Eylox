❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Premium and Creator Pass are billed monthly (or annually if you choose that option) and can be cancelled anytime from your Subscription settings. You'll keep your benefits until the end of the current billing period.

Is Eylux refundable?

Eylux coin purchases are generally final once the coins are delivered to your account, since they can be spent immediately. See our Refund Policy for the exceptions we do make.

What happens if I upgrade or downgrade mid-cycle?

Upgrading takes effect immediately and we prorate the difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrading takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle so you keep what you already paid for.

Which payment methods are supported?

We support major credit/debit cards and popular regional payment methods at checkout. Available options depend on your country.

Does cancelling Premium delete my progress?

No. Your account, coins, achievements, and game progress are always kept. Cancelling only removes the Premium-only perks (ad-free play, coin multiplier, etc.) going forward.

Can I gift Premium or Creator Pass to a friend?

Yes — gifting is available from the in-app Subscription page once you're signed in.

Are prices shown in my local currency?

We display an estimated price in your local currency based on your device settings. The exact amount charged, including any taxes, is confirmed at checkout.

Do the buttons on this page charge my card?

Not on this page — the buttons here are a demo preview of the purchase flow. Real purchases happen inside the app after you sign in.

Still have questions?

Our Help Center covers billing, accounts, and more — or reach out directly.
